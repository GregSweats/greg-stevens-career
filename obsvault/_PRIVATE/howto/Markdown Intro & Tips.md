	1. Default #keyboard-shortcut is `[CTRL]` + `[P]`.
	2. #BonusTip In [[vscode]] it is `[CTRL]` + `[SHIFT]` + `[P]`.
		1. [[Greg Stevens @gsteve3]] changed his shortcut to in vscode to match Obsidian.

		3. `CTRL + O` for `Go to file`
